Case Study of ABC

ABC is a Non-Governmental Organisation that provides Africa focused projects that support the Agriculture Industry, Transportation Industry, Health Industry, as well as other sustainability initiatives. ABC is a real organisation whose real name has been withheld to comply with obligations of confidentiality.

Using Microsoft Excel, I analyzed the Africa Portfolio dataset and created a set of PowerPoint slides to present key findings and trends to ABC's Senior Management Team. The slides included visualizations and insights on portfolio overview, project performance, sector analysis, geographic distribution, and financial performance. I also added brief talking points to each slide to guide my 10-minute presentation to the Management Team.

African Development Bank’s (AfDB) "Feed Africa" Project.

According to the SAPZ Brochure 2023, the African Development Bank’s (AfDB) agenda is revitalizing and re-invigorating African agriculture through its High 5, or priority area, known as “Feed Africa”. This information is readily available in the public space and so there is no obligation of confidentiality involved.

I was commissioned to perform an in-depth analysis of company data to identify key segments based on factors such as company size, home region, region of operation, value chain focus, investment capacity, and strategic priorities. The assignment further required the development of clear and distinct segment profiles to inform targeted outreach, the formulation of a private-sector engagement strategy to attract investment in Special Agro-Industrial Processing Zones (SAPZs), and the creation of a targeted outreach approach utilizing insights from the company data analysis.
